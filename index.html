Carbon Inventory follow ISO14064 & 14067

I'm proud to have been a key member of the Greenhouse Gas (GHG) inventory team at POSCO YAMATO VINA STEEL JSC (PY Vina), a leading Vietnamese steel joint venture. This project adhered to stringent international standards: ISO 14064 (for organizational GHG reporting) and ISO 14067 (for product carbon footprint).

This was critical to POSCO Group's sustainable development strategy, affirming their commitment to environmental impact reduction and fostering a "Better World with Green Steel." The Product Carbon Footprint (CFP) study focused on identifying, evaluating, and minimizing total emissions across the product lifecycle.

In my role, I was primarily responsible for synthesizing complex production data from factory operations, including raw materials, energy consumption, and transportation. I calculated GHG emissions strictly following ISO methods. To ensure data accuracy and representativeness, I applied advanced statistical methods like descriptive statistics and linear regression to determine key values.

The inventory reports and calculations were subsequently audited and verified by BSI, confirming their reliability and compliance.

To optimize workflows and enhance efficiency, I proactively developed an automated calculation dashboard based on the initial framework. This tool streamlined data collection and processing, significantly reducing manual errors and enabling continuous, effective monitoring of emission performance.
